2. Description of the App

Proof is a visit verification application that enables:

For Customers

  • Scanning QR codes at participating businesses
  • Entering verification words to create immutable visit records
  • Viewing personal visit history stored locally on device
  • Exporting and importing personal visit data

For Business Owners

  • Creating and managing business profiles
  • Generating QR codes for customer verification
  • Managing verification word systems
  • Accessing visit analytics and insights (with paid subscriptions)
  • Operating customer loyalty programs

The App creates cryptographically signed, timestamped records of customer visits to provide immutable proof of business interactions without requiring personal customer identification.
